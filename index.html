PLACES TO VISIT

Rome

Rome is the Eternal City where ancient Roman ruins, Renaissance masterpieces, and vibrant modern Italian life converge in a living museum that has served as the heart of Western civilization for nearly three millennia.

Rp. 7.999.000

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is located in Paris, France, on the Champ de Mars park in the city's 7th arrondissement, at the address 5 Avenue Anatole France.

Rp. 999.000

Fuji Mountain

Mount Fuji is a towering, iconic volcano located on the island of Honshu, Japan, straddling the prefectures of Yamanashi and Shizuoka.

Rp. 2.300.000

Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu is a breathtaking 15th-century Inca citadel perched high in the Peruvian Andes, featuring remarkable stone architecture that blends seamlessly with dramatic mountain peaks and clouds, making it one of the world's most iconic archaeological wonders.

Rp. 1.270.000

Bali

Bali is Indonesia's enchanting "Island of the Gods" where emerald rice terraces, ancient Hindu temples, pristine beaches, and a rich artistic culture combine with warm Balinese hospitality to create a tropical paradise that captivates millions of visitors each year.

Rp. 899.000

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a stunning white marble mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in the 17th century as an eternal monument of love for his wife Mumtaz Mahal, renowned worldwide for its perfect symmetry, intricate craftsmanship, and ethereal beauty that changes with the light of day.

Rp. 1.000.000

Grand Canyon

The Grand Canyon is a massive geological marvel carved by the Colorado River over millions of years, stretching 277 miles long and up to a mile deep, revealing stunning layers of colorful rock formations that tell Earth's ancient history and offer some of the most awe-inspiring vistas in the world.

Rp. 799.000

Santorini

Santorini is a stunning volcanic island in the Aegean Sea famous for its dramatic cliffside villages with whitewashed buildings and blue-domed churches, spectacular sunsets over the caldera, and breathtaking views that make it one of Greece's most romantic and photographed destinations.

Rp. 732.000
